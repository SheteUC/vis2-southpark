05

Presence Is Not Power

Kenny appears in 161 episodes but ranks 15th by words — the widest gap in the cast. Randy appears in 129 episodes but ranks 4th by words. Being present and owning the dialogue are very different things.

Left — rank by episode appearances  Right — rank by total words spoken  Lines slanting right = more words than their presence suggests; left = less.
06

Who Owns an Episode

Cartman's ceiling is 55.7% of an episode's words. Stan and Kyle hover near 30% at their peak — consistent but capped. Randy and Butters are detonators: when they're the focus, they take over completely.

Each dot = one episode the character appears in. Position = share of that episode's total words.

The Ensemble Machine

South Park runs on a recognisable cast architecture: anchors who hold every episode, spotlight characters who hijack episodes when called upon, and a deep supporting ensemble that provides continuity without demanding voice.
